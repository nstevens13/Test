The Census Survey Information Tool (CSIT) was created to serve as a repository of program level metadata in an easily accessible location for each of the Census Bureau’s business surveys.

The CSIT contains information such as: OMB numbers and expiration dates, the authority for collection, frequency of the survey, if it is mandatory or voluntary, type of collection activities (e.g. mail out dates, follow-up dates, etc.), form numbers, sample size, websites, and collection modes just to name a few.

The metadata provided is collected and maintained by the program area’s point of contact and updated periodically.
